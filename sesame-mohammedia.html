About Us

Our Story

At Sésame Restaurant, we are passionate about bringing the rich culinary traditions of Lebanon and Morocco to the heart of Mohammedia. Our journey began with a simple vision: to create a space where authentic flavors meet elegant dining.

Each dish on our menu is prepared with care using traditional recipes and the finest ingredients. Our chefs have mastered the art of blending spices and techniques from both Lebanese and Moroccan cuisines to create a unique dining experience.

Our restaurant offers a warm and inviting atmosphere where every detail has been carefully considered to ensure your comfort. From the elegant interior design to the attentive service, we strive to make every visit memorable.
