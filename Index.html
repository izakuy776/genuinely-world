ABOUT US

Welcome to Sportsmen's Bar & Grill

Sportsmen's Bar & Grill has been serving the Wilton community since 1995. We're a casual bar and grill known for our delicious steaks, burgers, and friendly atmosphere. Whether you're looking to catch the game, enjoy a meal with friends, or just relax with a cold drink, we've got you covered.

Our casual and welcoming environment makes us the perfect spot for lunch, dinner, or late-night drinks. With multiple TVs throughout the establishment, you'll never miss a moment of your favorite sporting events.

Delicious comfort food

OUR MENU

From juicy steaks to mouth-watering burgers and appetizers, our menu offers something for everyone. Pair your meal with one of our many beer options or a perfectly mixed cocktail.

Menu Highlights

Steaks

Our steaks are hand-selected, perfectly aged, and cooked to your preferred temperature.

  • Ribeye Steak - Juicy, marbled ribeye with your choice of sides
  • NY Strip - Tender strip steak seasoned to perfection
  • Sirloin - Lean and flavorful with house seasonings

Burgers

Our burgers are made with fresh ground beef, grilled to perfection, and served on a toasted bun.

  • Classic Cheeseburger - With American cheese and all the fixings
  • Bacon Burger - Topped with crispy bacon and cheddar
  • Mushroom Swiss - Sautéed mushrooms and melted Swiss

Appetizers

Perfect for sharing or starting your meal with a tasty treat.

  • Wings - Buffalo, BBQ, or dry-rubbed with ranch or blue cheese
  • Loaded Nachos - Topped with all your favorite fixings
  • Curly Fries - Seasoned and served with special dipping sauce

Drinks

Quench your thirst with our selection of beverages, from ice-cold beers to signature cocktails.

  • Domestic Beers - A wide selection of your favorites
  • Craft Beers - Rotating selection of local and regional brews
  • Cocktails - Classic and signature mixed drinks

Daily Specials

Don't miss our daily food and drink specials! From Wing Wednesday to Saturday Game Day specials featuring $0.50 wings, $2.50 domestic beers, and $2.50 well drinks.

Visit us on Saturdays for our famous Bloody Mary breakfast specials!
