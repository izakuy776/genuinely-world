Sky Bar Oriente Experience

On the 16th floor of the Tivoli Oriente Lisboa Hotel, Sky Bar Oriente offers breathtakingly exclusive views of the River Tagus and Lisbon's modern skyline.

Whether unwinding after a long day or seeking a sophisticated spot for business lunches, you can rise above the routine with masterful signature cocktails, sushi and other international delights.

Thanks to its central covered area, designed with cosmopolitan elegance, it has become a year-round must-visit destination in Lisbon.
